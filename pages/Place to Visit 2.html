Beaches in Myanmar

Ngapali Beach
 

The beautiful Ngapali Beach is located in Thandwe (Sandoway) about an hour's fight from Yangon. This unspoiled beach stretches over 3Km and is ideal place for everyone who loves sea, sand and sun or swimming , snorkeling or carefree Loitering on the beach, exploring or Just stilling amidst whistling wind.

 
 

Chaung Tha Beach
 

Chaungthar, newly developed beach is located 40 Km to the west of Pathein (Bassein) in Ayeyarwaddy Division. You may explore the neighboring villages to study the fishing Industry.

 
 

Ngwe Saung Beach
 

The long and beautiful beach stretches for 9 miles. It is deserted and features purest white sand and crystal-clear blue water.

 
 

Maung Magan Beach
 

Maung Magan Beach is long and shallow, located on the Taninthayi coast in the south of Myanmar. It is totally un-spoilt, but is currently under development. It is reached from Dawei, which is a 75-minute flight away from Yangon.
